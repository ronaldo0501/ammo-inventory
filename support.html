Frequently Asked Questions

Tap the + button in the top right corner of the Inventory screen. Select your category, choose your caliber from the dropdown, enter the quantity, and tap "Add to Inventory."

Tap the − button in the top right corner. Select the category and caliber you used, enter the quantity, and tap "Log Use." The app will only let you log rounds you actually have in inventory.

Swipe left on any ammo row in the inventory list. Tap the blue Edit button (sliders icon). Enter your desired threshold and tap "Save Threshold." You'll see a warning badge on that row whenever it drops to or below that number.

Swipe left on any ammo row and tap the red Delete button. You'll be asked to confirm before it's removed.

Your inventory is stored locally on your device. It will be included in your standard iOS device backup (iCloud or iTunes). If you delete the app your inventory data will be lost, so make sure you have device backups enabled.

Ammo Inventory is free to download and supported by ads from Google AdMob. A banner ad appears at the bottom of the inventory screen and occasional full-screen ads may appear after logging transactions. This keeps the app free for everyone.

We're always looking to expand our caliber list. Please use the contact form below to request a caliber and we'll consider adding it in a future update.
